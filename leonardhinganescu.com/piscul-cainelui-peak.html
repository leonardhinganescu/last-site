Piscul Câinelui Peak (1650 m)

Route 13 (blue dot) connects the city of Sinaia and Piscul Câinelui Peak (1650 m) from the Baiului ridge, climbing to the top of Piscul Câinelui. Beautiful views of the Bucegi Massif, part of the Baiului ridge, Cumpătu Mountain, V. Câinelui and others. The photos were taken with the Canon A1 on the Ilford HP5 Plus 400 and Kodak ColorPlus 200.
